Requirements:
Old Students

• Must pass the interview and entrance exam for the course applied for
• Must meet prescribed average rating for the course applied for
• Official Transcript of Records for evaluation purposes
• Honorable Dismissal
• Copy of Live Birth Certificate (Authenticated by NSO)


Freshmen

• Must pass the interview and entrance exam for the course applied for
• Must meet prescribed average rating for the course applied for
• Form 138 – High School Card or
• Form 137-A Permanent Record
• Certificate of Good Moral Character
• 2 copies of recent 1x1 ID picture
• Copy of Live Birth Certificate (Authenticated by NSO)

Transferee

• Must have a copy of his/her grades of all subjects of the previous semester. In case of failures, dropped subjects, or NG, he/she is subject to the retention policy of his/her college and will require the concerned approval for enrolment.
